Boxing Day Tour

Wantsum Morris have been dancing out on Boxing Day since 1967 visiting Birchington-on-sea, St. Nicholas-at-wade, Marshside and Sarre. We missed one year due to bad weather but got back out a week later.

The schedule:

11:00 The Powell Arms, Birchington-on-sea
12:00 Bell Inn, St. Nicholas-at-wade
13:00 The Gate Inn, Marshside
14:00 Crown Inn (Cherry Brandy House), Sarre

For more information: bagman@wantsum-morris.org.uk or see the event details on Facebook
